CALL ME

Boss Revolution CALL ME offers customers a Local Landline Number in their home country that is forwarded to their US phone number, so that their family and friends can call them at a local call rate.

Overview

  • Customer receives a Local Number that is forwarded to their US phone number.
  • Unlimited inbound calling to the US.
  • Number is available for 30 days.
  • Plan can be renewed at the retailers location.
  • After plan expiration customer has 14 days to renew and get the same number back. Otherwise the number is lost and they would need to get a new Local Number.

FAQ

  • How long does it take to get a response on an application?
  • It can take up to 10 business days to get a response. If the application is missing information it will take longer.
  • How long does the compliance training take?
  • It takes approximately 45 minutes.
  • Do we get a Certificate when we complete the training?
  • Yes.
  • We are trying to register for compliance but nothing shows up?
  • You may need to wait up to 2 business days from when you are approved before your can register.
  • What should we do if we need to update our banking information?
  • You must complete the bank change form and send it to CFP. Once CFP has approved the change then you can change the banking information.
  • How does Bill Pay effect my Boss revolution wallet?
  • Bill Payment will not have any effect on your available credit or cash wallet.
  • Can I process a same day payment?
  • No.
  • Are payments delivered on weekends or Holidays?
  • No Payments are not delivered on weekends and bank or other holidays.
  • Can I cancel a payment?
  • Yes retailers can cancel a payment up until 7:00 PM Eastern Time of the same day that the payment was processed.
  • How often do I need to deposit the money in to my account?
  • Retailers must deposit everyday excluding weekends and bank or other holidays.

CALL ME

Boss Revolution CALL ME offers customers a Local Landline Number in their home country that is forwarded to their US phone number, so that their family and friends can call them at a local call rate.

Overview

  • Customer receives a Local Number that is forwarded to their US phone number.
  • Unlimited inbound calling to the US.
  • Number is available for 30 days.
  • Plan can be renewed at the retailers location.
  • After plan expiration customer has 14 days to renew and get the same number back. Otherwise the number is lost and they would need to get a new Local Number.

FAQ

  • Do my friends and family abroad have to pay for the call?

    CALL ME does not charge your family and friends any extra fee. However your family and friends local calling plans will apply, the same as if they are calling someone in their home country.

  • Do I have any additional fees to the incoming call?

    CALL ME does not charge you any additional fees. However your local plan rates/minutes will apply as if you are receiving a local incoming call.

  • Can I change the U.S. forwarding number?

    Yes. The U.S. forwarding number can be changed 3 times each month (only twice in the first month).

  • Can I have two CALL ME numbers?

    Each customer can have only one CALL ME number to a specified U.S. number at a time.

  • Can I manage my CALL ME account online?

    No. CALL ME numbers can be managed by a Boss Revolution retailer.

  • Can I return a CALL ME plan?

    This product is not refundable. If you do not renew your plan after 30 days the service will be disconnected.

  • Can I keep the same local number forever?

    As long as you renew your plan within 14 days from plan expiration you will be able to keep the same number.

  • How do I know when my plan expires?

    Your plan expires 30 days from plan activation. We will send you txt messages reminding you of your plan expiration date and to renew your plan.
